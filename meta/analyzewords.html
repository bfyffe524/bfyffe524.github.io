Analyze words

Analyze Words is a web app based on the Linguistic Inquiry and Word Count (LIWC) program. This app is different from the others as it doesn't offer the same analytical features. The app's aim is to analyze the tweets of a Twitter handle to define the personality of the user.

The Good:

  • Easy to use
  • It can be used for free
  • No login required

The (not so) Bad:

  • Data is not shared with 3rd parties, but no Terms & Conditions available

The Ugly:

  • Not optimized for tablets and mobile phones
  • No customer support

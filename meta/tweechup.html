Tweechup

Tweechup is a sleek free twitter analytic app. Their motto is "made with <3 for you". It offers basic twitter analytic functionality in a snazzy and responsive package. It focuses on single user analysis and in drawing the connections people have on twitter. Similarly to the basic Twitter Analytics it does not offer data extraction but just viewing.

The Good:

  • Very good UI design and responsiveness
  • It's free
  • Good support system and customer service
  • Interactive maps are a pleasure to use

The (not so) Bad:

  • Does not have instructions or help tips
  • Canadian law applies to its data retention policies

The Ugly:

  • What happens to your data is a secret
  • The data analysed cannot be exported
